- Task: I needed to standardize local environments and CI/CD to reduce environment drift and onboarding time across the delivery team.
- Action: I owned the deployment pipelines and Drupal/PHP update cycles, introducing Capistrano automation and CI/CD tooling to achieve zero-downtime releases.
- Result: Environment-related regression incidents dropped by approximately 30% across release cycles.
- Situation: Enterprise clients needed backend services built on Laravel that could integrate cleanly with multiple external systems.
- Task: The team needed reliable REST and SOAP integrations connecting CMS platforms with third-party services across up to 5 concurrent client projects.
- Action: I developed and maintained Laravel-based REST API services using OOP/MVC principles, PSR coding standards, and Eloquent ORM, integrating 10+ third-party APIs including payment gateways, CRMs, and data feeds.
- Result: Delivered clean, testable back-end code at production scale for enterprise clients.
- Situation: At [empty] Studio, 10+ client sites were compromised, putting production uptime and data integrity at risk.

- Action: I performed proactive security incident response - malware removal, vulnerability identification, and remediation - restoring each site to full production.
- Result: Achieved zero recurrence of the security incidents while preserving client uptime and data integrity across the affected portfolio.
